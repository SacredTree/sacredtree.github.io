About Us

Thanks for stopping by our site. We're a husband and wife team that started collecting antiques to furnish our own home years ago. We really love the hunt for "treasure" and to give neglected antiques a new life. In 2008 we opened Sacred Tree Antiques and Collectibles in Notus, Idaho. Before too long we needed more space and we found it in Vale, Oregon. After many months of renovation, in 2013 we moved to our current location in Vale. We love all the different people we meet and we enjoy sharing our love of antiques. We hope you stop by and find a treasured piece to take to your home.

FAQ's

  • Q. Do you do consignment? A. We rarely have extra room to take on consignment items.
  • Q. Why Vale Oregon? A. Why not? We've always loved small town communities.
  • Q. Will you buy my _____? A. We occasionally buy individual items that we think will sell quickly. Please stop by or call to talk about it.
  • Q. What kind of antiques do you have in stock? A. Take a look at our gallery for examples of what we typically have in the store. We bring in new items at least weekly.
  • Q. Can you restore my dresser/table/chair etc? A. We offer some furniture refinishing and we'd love to discuss your needs with you.
