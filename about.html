About Us

Thanks for stopping by our site. We're Jerry and Cheryl, a husband and wife team, that started collecting antiques to furnish our own home years ago. We really love the hunt for unique "treasure" and to give neglected antiques a new life. In 2008 we opened Sacred Tree Antiques and Collectibles in Notus, Idaho. Before too long we needed more space and we found it in Vale, Oregon. After many months of renovation, in 2013 we moved to our current location at 293 Washington Street in Vale. We love all the different people we meet and we enjoy sharing our love of antiques. We hope you stop by and find a special piece to take to your home.

FAQ's

  • Q. Do you do consignments? A. We're fortunate to find enough antiques to fill our store at the present time.
  • Q. Why Vale Oregon? A. Why not? We've always loved small town communities.
  • Q. Will you buy my _____? A. We occasionally buy items that are a good fit for our store but items need to be a fair and reasonable price. Please stop by or call to talk about your item.
  • Q. What kind of antiques do you carry in your store? A. Take a look at our gallery for examples of what we typically have in the store. We bring in new "old" items daily.
  • Q. Can you restore my dresser/table/chair etc? A. Yes, we offer furniture refinishing and we'd love to discuss your item and needs with you.
